About Us

Rolex is a Swiss watch designer and manufacturer based in Geneva, Switzerland. It was founded in 1905 as Wilsdorf and Davis by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London. The company registered Rolex as the brand name of its watches in 1908 and became Rolex Watch Co. Ltd. in 1915. Rolex is the leading name in luxury wristwatches and created the world's first waterproof watch in 1926. It has a major presence in the sports world with endorsements in golf, motor sports, tennis and yachting.
